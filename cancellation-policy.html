Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2026

We understand that plans can change. This policy outlines our cancellation, rescheduling, and no-show terms so you know what to expect.

Cancellation and Rescheduling

Notice given Fee
More than 24 hours before the appointment No charge. Cancel or reschedule freely via Fresha.
Less than 24 hours before the appointment A cancellation fee of up to 50% of the service price may apply.
No-show (no contact, failure to attend) The full service fee may be charged.

How to Cancel or Reschedule

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment at any time via the Fresha booking platform using the link in your confirmation email. If you are unable to do so online, please contact your studio location directly as early as possible.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive late, your treatment time may be reduced to avoid delays for other clients. The full service price will still apply. Please aim to arrive a few minutes before your scheduled appointment.

Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that genuine emergencies happen. If you need to cancel at short notice due to illness, a family emergency, or other unavoidable circumstances, please contact us as soon as possible. We will consider each situation individually.

Fresha Booking Platform

All bookings are managed through Fresha. Cancellation deadlines and any applicable fees will be displayed at the time of booking. By confirming your booking, you acknowledge and agree to this cancellation policy.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, please contact your nearest studio via the Fresha booking page or visit us in person.
